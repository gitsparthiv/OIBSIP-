About Ratan Tata

Ratan Tata, born on December 28, 1937, is a renowned Indian industrialist and philanthropist. He served as the chairman of the Tata Group, where he transformed the organization into a global powerhouse, driving innovations like the Tata Nano and acquisitions like Jaguar Land Rover.

Beyond business, he is celebrated for his philanthropic contributions through the Tata Trusts, supporting education, healthcare, and rural development.
